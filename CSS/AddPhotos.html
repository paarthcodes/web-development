A Trip Around the Globe

Africa

Republic of the Congo

  • Official Language: French
  • Currency: Central African CFA franc (XAF)
  • Fun Fact: Established Independence from France in 1960

Egypt

  • Official Language: Arabic
  • Currency: Egytian Pound
  • Fun Fact: The ancient Egyptians invented the 365-day calendar by adding five extra days to the 12-month, 360-day lunar cycle.

Asia

India

  • Official Language: Hindi
  • Currency: Rupee
  • Fun Fact: India is the origin of many important concepts and games, such as the number zero, chess, and the board game Snakes and Ladders.

Japan

  • Official Language: Japeneese
  • Currency: Japanese Yen
  • Fun Fact: A fun fact about Japan is that it has the world's oldest company, Kongo Gumi, founded in 578 AD.
