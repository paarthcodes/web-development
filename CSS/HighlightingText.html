Syllabus: Help-Dog Training

Course Content

By the end of this class, you will be able to guide your human across the street, grab a water bottle from the fridge, and open and close doorsl

Course Location

We will meet Mondays and Wednesdays at 4:30pm at the local municipal dog park. Bring snacks and water.

Assignments

There will be three tests you must pass to pass this course. The three tests will cover opening and closing doors, getting random objects out of the fridge, and successfully crossing a neighborhood street.
