Cherry Tree

Types of Cherries

Not all cherry trees produce edible cherries. Cherries can be sweet or sour. Some cherries are merely ornamental. Chokecherries are named cherry, but they're not actually cherries at all!. Cherry can also refer to a type of reddish wood.

Location

Cherry trees can be found in Europe, western Asia, some of Africa, and Australia. Interestingly, cherries were brought to England by order of Henry VIII and brought to North America by Dutch colonists.
